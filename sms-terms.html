Qortana offers SMS messaging services to provide business updates, appointment reminders, service notifications, and promotional offers to customers who have opted in to receive text messages. This program is designed to enhance your experience and keep you informed about our services.

Consent & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to receive text messages from Qortana, you expressly consent to receive automated text messages from us at the phone number provided. This may include:

  • Promotional messages about our services and offers
  • Service updates and announcements
  • Appointment reminders and confirmations

Important: Consent is not a condition of purchase. You can access our services without subscribing to text messages. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying to any message with one of the following keywords:

STOP • QUIT • END • CANCEL • UNSUBSCRIBE • OPT-OUT

After you opt out, you will receive one final confirmation message acknowledging your request. Once processed, you will no longer receive SMS messages from Qortana unless you re-opt-in.

We will honor all opt-out requests within ten (10) business days as required by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). In most cases, opt-outs are processed immediately.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions and preferences. You may receive:

We respect your time and inbox. We will not send excessive messages or spam.
